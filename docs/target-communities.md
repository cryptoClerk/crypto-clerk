# Target Crypto Freelancer Communities

## 1. Upwork
- **Why:** Added USDC payouts in 2025
- **Size:** Millions of freelancers
- **How to reach:** Upwork community forums, blog posts about crypto payments
- **Angle:** "Getting paid in USDC? Here's how to generate professional receipts and invoices"

## 2. Fiverr
- **Why:** Added USDC payouts in early 2026
- **Size:** Large international freelancer base
- **How to reach:** Fiverr community, seller forums
- **Angle:** "Crypto payment documentation for Fiverr sellers"

## 3. Deel
- **Why:** Added crypto payouts in 2024 via BVNK partnership
- **Size:** Enterprise-focused but growing freelancer base
- **How to reach:** Deel community, webinars
- **Angle:** "Deel pays you in USDC? Track it properly with CryptoClerks"

## 4. Cryptwerk Freelance Directory
- **Why:** Dedicated crypto freelance marketplace
- **Size:** Niche but highly targeted
- **How to reach:** List in their directory, partner with them
- **Angle:** Direct integration pitch for crypto freelancers

## 5. Reddit Communities
- **r/ethdev** - Ethereum developers (40K+ members)
- **r/cryptocurrency** - General crypto (7M+ members)
- **r/freelance** - General freelancers (500K+ members)
- **r/web3** - Web3 developers
- **How to reach:** Value-add posts, AMAs, helpful tools
- **Angle:** "I built a tool to generate professional receipts from crypto payments"

## 6. Discord Communities
- **Developer DAO** - Web3 developers
- **BuildSpace** - Web3 builders
- **Various NFT/DeFi project Discords** - Communities with freelancers
- **How to reach:** Share in relevant channels, offer value
- **Angle:** "Free tool for freelancers getting paid in crypto"

## 7. Twitter/X
- **Web3 freelancers** - Many advertise services on X
- **Crypto Twitter** - General audience
- **How to reach:** Threads, replies to relevant tweets, partnerships
- **Angle:** Share auto-receipt generation, invoice features

## 8. Telegram
- **Crypto freelance groups** - Various regional groups
- **Web3 developer groups** - Technical communities
- **How to reach:** Share in groups, create bot integration
- **Angle:** "Bot that generates receipts from tx hashes"

## 9. Indie Hackers
- **Why:** Community of solo builders and freelancers
- **Size:** Active community of independent makers
- **How to reach:** Product launches, Show IH posts
- **Angle:** "Launching the operating system for crypto payments"

## 10. Hacker News
- **Why:** Technical audience, early adopters
- **Size:** Large tech community
- **How to reach:** Show HN post, thoughtful comments
- **Angle:** "Show HN: Auto-generate receipts from blockchain payments"

## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1 (Now)
1. Reddit posts in r/ethdev and r/freelance about the tool
2. Twitter thread about the problem and solution
3. Indie Hackers Show IH post
4. Share in relevant Discord communities

### Phase 2 (After Launch)
1. Apply to Cryptwerk directory
2. Partner with Upwork/Fiverr community content
3. Create tutorials for Deel users
4. Build Telegram bot for viral growth

### Phase 3 (Scale)
1. Sponsor relevant newsletters (Web3, freelance)
2. Influencer partnerships in crypto Twitter
3. YouTube tutorial content
4. Podcast appearances about crypto business tools
