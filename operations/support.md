## FAQ (Draft)

**Q: Do you store my private keys?**
A: No. We only store public wallet addresses. We never ask for private keys or seed phrases.

**Q: Is my transaction data private?**
A: Blockchain data is public by nature. We only access what is already public. Your labels and descriptions are private to your account.

**Q: How accurate are the USD values?**
A: We use historical price data from CoinGecko. Values are approximate and for reference only. Consult your tax professional for exact figures.

**Q: Can I use this for tax filing?**
A: Our reports are designed to help you and your accountant. We are not tax software and do not provide tax advice.

**Q: What chains do you support?**
A: MVP supports Ethereum and Polygon. BSC, Arbitrum, and Optimism coming soon.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Pro and Business subscriptions can be canceled anytime. We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you're not satisfied.

## Escalation Path

1. **User hits issue**
2. Checks FAQ/docs → self-resolves
3. If not, emails support@cryptobooks.app
4. Founder responds within 24-48h
5. If bug, added to GitHub issues
6. If feature request, added to backlog
7. User notified when resolved
